Meet Your Artist

Passionate about creating beautiful memories through the art of mehndi

Hello, I'm Sneha

With a passion for henna artistry that spans years of dedicated practice, I specialize in creating stunning mehndi designs that celebrate your most precious moments. From intricate bridal patterns that tell your love story to elegant Arabic designs perfect for modern celebrations, each piece is crafted with precision, care, and natural henna for lasting beauty.

My journey in mehndi art began with a deep appreciation for traditional Indian designs, and has evolved to include contemporary styles that blend classic elegance with modern sophistication. Every design is personalized to reflect your unique style and the significance of your occasion.

Our Services

Comprehensive mehndi services for every occasion and style preference

Bridal Mehndi

Exquisite full-hand and arm designs featuring traditional motifs, personalized elements, and intricate patterns. Perfect for your special day with 4-6 hour application time for deep, rich color that lasts.

Engagement Mehndi

Elegant designs perfect for engagement ceremonies. Beautiful patterns that complement your celebration while maintaining sophistication. Customizable to match your style and preferences.

Arabic Mehndi

Modern, minimalist designs with flowing patterns and negative space. Perfect for contemporary celebrations, parties, and those who prefer elegant simplicity with a touch of sophistication.

Traditional Indian

Classic Rajasthani and Mughal-inspired designs with mandalas, paisleys, and intricate lattice work. Timeless patterns that honor cultural heritage while adding modern elegance.

Festival & Events

Quick and beautiful designs for Karva Chauth, Eid, Diwali, and other festive occasions. Stunning patterns that can be completed in 1-2 hours, perfect for busy schedules.

Custom Designs

Personalized mehndi designs tailored to your vision. Collaborate with me to create unique patterns that incorporate meaningful symbols, names, or themes special to you.
